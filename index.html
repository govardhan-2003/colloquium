About Colloquium

Colloquium 2025 is aimed to bring together academicians, scientists, professionals, engineers, and industrialists specializing in various fields of engineering around the globe on a single platform. Many leading scientists and researchers from our country are expected to attend this conference. Thus, the conference will serve as an ideal inational level forum to discover recent technical advances, exchange information/ideas/experiences, and germinate innovations exploring new aspects of the existing technology and its future application

About SCTCE

Sree Chitra Thirunal College of Engineering (SCTCE), Thiruvananthapuram was established by the Govt. of Kerala in the year 1995 in memoriam of the Great Maharaja of Travancore who is the mastermind behind the temple entry proclamation and is affiliated to the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University(KTU) of Kerala. SCT is the first college in South India to offer a B.Tech. programme in Automobile Engineering and it was one of the few colleges that got accredited in early 2000. The governing body of the college is controlled by the Government of Kerala. It is chaired by the Minister for Transport, with the Higher Education Secretary, the Finance Secretary, the Transport Secretary and the Managing Director of the Kerala State Road Transport Corporation as ex officio members. Two additional members of the governing body are educational experts nominated by the chairperson. The college is in Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of Kerala. It is situated at Pappanamcode, on one side of the Trivandrum – Kanyakumari highway. The college is 5 km from the Thiruvananthapuram Central railway station and about 10 km from the Thiruvananthapuram International Airport. The college is easily accessible by road from any part of Thiruvananthapuram.
